Who are these handsome men?

Stray Kids is a globally popular K-pop group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017 by a survivial show of the same name.

The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan (leader), Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Their success mainly comes from their self-produced label; their producing unit, 3RACHA, consisting of CB97 (Bang Chan), SPEARB (Changbin), and J.One (Han), creates music of various genres in-house. Other units include DanceRacha (Lee Know, Hyunjin, and Felix) and VocalRacha (Seungmin and I.N.)

While there are designated positions such as vocalist, rapper, and dancer, all of the members are true all-rounders. One example is Changbin, whose ferocious rap contrasts beautifully with his soothing vocals. His performance on King of Masked Singer (Episodes 261-262) was truly amazing. Another member, Han, also appeared on King of Masked Singer in episode 197, and his singing actially introduced me to another artist! One day, I hope Bang Chan will appear on the show as well, so that all of 3RACHA will have participated.
